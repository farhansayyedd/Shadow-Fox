JOURNEY

KEY MILESTONES

2008
RCB founded — The franchise debuts in the inaugural IPL season. A 19-year-old Virat Kohli is part of the first squad under Rahul Dravid.
2009
First Final — RCB reach the IPL final in South Africa, going down to the Deccan Chargers.
2011
Chris Gayle arrives — The Universe Boss joins mid-season and transforms RCB into the most feared batting lineup. They finish as runners-up.
2013
Virat Kohli becomes captain — Begins the longest captaincy stint in RCB history. Gayle smashes 175* off 66 balls, the highest IPL score ever.
2016
Kohli's 973-run season — The greatest individual batting season in IPL history. RCB reach the final but fall to SRH.
2025
EE SALA CUP NAMDE! 🏆 — Under captain Rajat Patidar and coach Andy Flower, RCB win their maiden IPL title, defeating Punjab Kings in the final. The 17-year wait is over!
2026
Defending Champions — RCB enter IPL 2026 with a gold star on their jersey, ready to defend the title.
